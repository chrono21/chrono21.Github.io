my project

Crafted with precision and innovation, the prosthetic arm featured in this portfolio represents a pinnacle of technological advancement and human-centred design. Each component is meticulously engineered to seamlessly integrate with the user's body, providing not just functionality, but a sense of empowerment and freedom. From the anatomically contoured socket to the advanced myoelectric control system, every aspect is tailored to enhance mobility and restore independence. With a focus on both aesthetics and functionality, this prosthetic arm embodies a harmonious fusion of form and purpose, symbolizing the boundless possibilities of modern prosthetics in improving lives and redefining abilities.

proposal of Prosthetic ARM

The objective of this project is to develop a prosthetic arm with a robotic elbow joint that is highly articulated. The arm should have a range of motion of 90 degrees to the left and right, as well as up and down. The goal is to create a basic prosthetic arm that utilizes motors or servos to allow for a limited range of motion.
